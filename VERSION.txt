Current Version Snapshot:
Public Research Release — v1.0

Versioning Model:
This project does not follow semantic or product-style versioning.
Version identifiers represent meaningful public-facing stages of
ethical framing, research clarity, accessibility design, and archival maturity.

This version reflects:
- initial public release of the HADES research website
- establishment of Agent Reliability & Rehabilitation Engineering (ARRE)
- formal articulation of mission, ethics, and governance stance
- static, accessible, archival-safe site architecture
- clarity-first communication and reflective presentation

Future versions may represent:
- editorial refinement phases
- accessibility enhancements
- ethical or methodological clarifications
- structural improvements to research presentation

Version tag purpose:
to support archival reference, citation stability,
institutional cataloging, and historical interpretation.
